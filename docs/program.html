A 10-Week Immersion in Data Analysis

Every engagement with the digital world generates data. Structured or unstructured, these huge data sets can reveal useful patterns, trends, and information about people and their habits. The demand for workers with expertise in analyzing data is high.

Join us for 10 full-day sessions of this highly intensive course where you’ll gain a comprehensive understanding of the critical and practical elements of big data analytics. Offered in an applied format, the program includes practice and lab components to address the application of big data sets in real-world situations.

You’ll gain a comprehensive understanding of the critical and practical elements of big data analytics. The program includes hands-on practice and lab components to address the applications of big data in real-world simulations. Topics include data structure, warehousing, data mining and analysis, pattern recognition, trends analysis, data relevancy, data modeling, predictive and descriptive analytics, data visualization techniques, and more. Students will utilize analytical tools and products such as Splunk and gain familiarity with the applications of these products.

Big data applications are commonplace. Most large companies have one or more of these applications, providing fast access to large stores of customer and sales data. As the IT organization grows to install, support and maintain these applications, new job categories and new tasks are added to the mix. These can include:

  • big data hardware and software support
  • business analysts (using analytics to probe and explore the data)
  • managers who must supervise and prioritize job tasks

Hardware/software support positions often center on the technical nature of big data. The emphasis of our program focuses on the analyst and manager roles regarding business management of big data.
