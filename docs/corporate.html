Our Training Expertise

Emory Corporate Learning creates client value through the application of our skills, experience and expertise to develop and deliver relevant learning solutions to enhance the organization's employee performance. We support our clients by providing high-quality, relevant and practical talent development programs in a variety of subject areas that can be customized to meet unique organizational needs.

Courses can be tailored and delivered onsite to corporate clients. Please call Bob Poole at 404-727-9479 or email us for more information.

Certificate Training Programs

Emory Corporate Learning can deliver the following programs to your organization:

  • New Manager Program
  • Senior Manager Program
  • Leadership Development Program
  • Business Communication Program
  • PHR/SPHR Program
  • Project Management Program Certificate
  • Lean Six Sigma

Contact Bob Poole, Business Development Manager, at 404-727-9379 or email us to learn more about our certificate programs and how they can support your talent management needs.

Emory Corporate Learning instructors are subject-matter experts (SMEs) who provide practical business solutions anchored in relevant adult-learning principles. Our SMEs combine appropriate theoretical foundations with valuable business experience to ensure realistic knowledge and skill transfer.

Their expertise is derived from a combination of education (advanced degrees in adult education leadership, management, business administration, etc.), formal training and relevant work experience. Our instructors engage participants in skill development through expert delivery and facilitation of our training solutions.

Collectively our instructors have held executive, mid-management, and consulting positions in talent management, organizational effectiveness, human resources, and operations.

As a leader in corporate and professional education in the Southeast, Emory Corporate Learning programs provide hands-on, need-specific, learning solutions to small, medium, and large organizations in the region and beyond.

Our clients all share a common need for high-quality, cost-effective and proactive professional employee development and learning solutions; they depend on Emory Corporate Learning to maximize the return on their training investment by helping employees reach their potential.

Contact us to find out more about the benefits of partnering with Emory Corporate Learning.
